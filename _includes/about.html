About Yeji

I'm a Product Manager. It's a bit of a loaded title for those who are (and aren't) in the know. Broadcasting my imposter syndrome to the world, I see myself iterating and refining the domain and success of my role to date. Here is my attempt.

You hear of successful products suddenly "catching on." Behind them are people who took the fluffy idea-in-the-sky and brought it to earth by identifying a problem, a user, and a solution through continued learning and feedback. That's my domain as a Product Manager. I strive to help make that happen.

Outside of work, I like to collect instruments, fountain pens and inks, books, kitchen gadgets, board games, foreign language workbooks, video games, and lego sets. I also enjoy setting up self-improvement goals by tricking myself into gamifying the tasks.
