About Yeji

I'm a Product Manager. It's a bit of a loaded title. Below is my latest attempt at explaining what I do.

You hear of successful products suddenly "catching on." Behind the scenes, someone needs to take the fluffy pie-in-the-sky ideas and bring it to earth by identifying a user problem, a target user, and a solution through continued learning and feedback. Behind the ideas, there are evaluation that must be done to understand whether the idea is feasible to realize, viable for the business model and operational constraints, and valuable for the customers where their problems are solved ethically.

That's my domain as a Product Manager. I strive to help make that happen.
